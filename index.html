03 — In Progress

Traçe

Your Trace. Your Style.

Work in progress — early product screens

Traçe is a fashion marketplace concept where independent brands and stores list their products for people to discover and buy — built around two core innovations.

AI Style Scan

For people who feel they lack a defined personal style, Traçe reads a full-body scan alongside a short lifestyle questionnaire. Combining physical proportions with personal preferences, the AI generates tailored style recommendations — each linked directly to the piece for purchase.

Virtual Fit Preview

Users can save a full-body photo with their exact measurements to preview any garment on the platform on their own body before buying — removing the guesswork from online fashion shopping.

Community

Beyond shopping, users share their outfits for feedback and follow a feed of style inspiration and fashion education from other members — every look posted carries tappable tags linking straight to the pieces worn.
